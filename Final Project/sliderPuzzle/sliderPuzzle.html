Welcome to the Slider Puzzle game!

The purpose of this game is to try slide all the pieces in the correct slots (reference upper right picture).

Simply click a square near an empty slot to move it.

Press the reset button to randomize the pieces.

For future versions I plan to add more images, a timed mode, & a "puzzle complete" screen.
